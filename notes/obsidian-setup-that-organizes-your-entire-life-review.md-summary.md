# Obsidian Setup That Organizes Your Entire Life Review - Summary

This short video recommends a simple Obsidian life-organization setup built around the PARA method and a few workflow plugins.

## Main Setup

Use the PARA folder structure:

- `Projects`
  - Active efforts with specific outcomes.

- `Areas`
  - Ongoing responsibilities and life domains.

- `Resources`
  - Reference material, ideas, and useful knowledge.

- `Archive`
  - Completed, inactive, or old material.

## Recommended Plugins

Install plugins that support automation and routine capture:

- `Templater`
- `Calendar`
- `Daily Notes`

## Templates

Create smart templates for recurring note types, especially:

- Meetings
- Projects
- Daily notes

The templates should automatically populate dates and other repeated fields.

## Linking

Use tags and backlinks to connect notes across folders. This turns the vault from a folder system into a personal knowledge management system.

## Main Takeaway

The suggested setup is: organize with PARA, automate with templates and daily-note plugins, and connect everything with tags and backlinks.
